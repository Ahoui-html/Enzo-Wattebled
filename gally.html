Gally is a vocal assistant made in collaboration with the French insurer MAIF.

The aim of this vocal assistant was to ease the access to all theMAIF’s juridic, social content and videos destined to teachers.

Indeed they often face delicate situations: relations between student or/and parents, harassment … Gally plays the role of confident bound for young teachers who finds themself in an unknown situation. After the conversation, Gally offers documents, pieces of information, or videos linked to the teacher’s situation to guide him in his process
