This academic project have been realized during the first year of my Bachelor's degree.

« Les Utopiales » is a French Science Fiction festival in Nantes. It's not a regular festival with cosplay, superheroes, and spaceship everywhere. The festival is known to abord the science fiction through complex thematics and invites philosophers and thinkers to discuss about the theme of the year. Those conferences are quite deep, and the objective here was to prepare the user to understand key points of the year's thematic. This project was also an opportunity to develop a "wow" graphic aspect, we were free to experiment with any design we wanted
