Made during a workshop of one week, this interactive installation runs strange attractor functions to generate visuals.

The graphic part is linked to an autogenerated audio, using chaotic values of the function. The result is a deep and chaotic visual and audio landscape reacting to the user input.

The book, “The theory of chaos“ by James Gleick was our principal source of inspiration in this project.
