Realized during my internship at Ghostraum (Berlin), this installation comes from the idea to create an immersive and interactive shadow theatre.

The final prototype offers a journey to the spectator where he can see himself moving into a shady world surrounded with soft lights and natural elements.

It was a real opportunity to apply my technical knowledge (PureData, Arduino) earned from my last years at school.
