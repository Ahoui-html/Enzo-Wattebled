"Amply" is my Bachelor's project, an application about music and network creation. Through this project, I wanted to help musicians finding other people to meet each other, to play together (for example if you want to practice your drums in a band), if you need to find a beatmaker, etc.

The project also integrates a professional dimension where musicians artists can easily contact independent labels signed in the application. The app is still a prototype.
